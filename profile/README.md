## Welcome to EunoiaLife — A Revolution for the Neuro-divergence 

Eunoia is not another general wellness app.
It’s built for people dealing with OCD, anxiety, ADHD, intrusive thoughts, and urges — especially when managing things alone get worse.

💚 Eunoia helps you pause, understand, and respond differently — without shame, pressure, or overwhelming techniques.

---
### 🔍 URGE SEARCH ENGINE — DON’T GOOGLE IT
When an urge hits, searching online often fuels it.
Eunoia lets you search the urge itself and get calm, grounded explanations with gentle ways to pause instead of reacting.

- Understand why the urge feels so strong
- Learn how urges rise and fall on their own
- Get simple, non-overwhelming ways to wait it out

---

### ❓ WHAT IF LAB — FACE “WHAT IF?” THOUGHTS SAFELY

Intrusive thoughts and worst-case fears can loop endlessly.

The What If Lab helps you:
- Name what your brain is predicting
- Sit with uncertainty one step at a time
- Practice gentle ERP-style responses without clinical language

No lectures.
No pressure.
Just one safe step forward.

---

### 🎧📖 AUDIO LESSONS — AT YOUR OWN PACE

Low-energy days still count.

Listen to short, focused audiobooks about:
- OCD, anxiety, and intrusive thoughts
- ADHD patterns and focus struggles
- Learning to respond instead of react

Pause, rewind, or continue whenever you want.

---

### ⚔️ ADHD-FRIENDLY TASKS (SIDE QUESTS)

Traditional to-do lists can feel overwhelming.

Eunoia’s Side Quests are:
- Small
- Flexible
- Judgment-free
- If todo lists could talk ?!

Pending tasks gently remind you without guilt, using subtle animations and haptics to keep things moving.

---

### 🌱 PROGRESS WITHOUT PRESSURE

No streak punishment.
No “you failed” screens.

Eunoia tracks growth in a way that feels encouraging — not demanding.

---

### 🔒 PRIVATE BY DESIGN

- Chats are private
- No social comparison
- Built for moments you don’t want to explain to anyone else
